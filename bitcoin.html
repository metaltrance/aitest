⚡ Bitcoin: Odyssey of Digital Gold ⚡

Amid 2008's crumbling empires of debt and deceit, Satoshi Nakamoto unleashed Bitcoin—the genesis flame igniting a borderless rebellion. From a single whitepaper, it spawned the blockchain: a tamper-proof vault of truth, powered by miners' relentless computational sieges.

Defying tyrants and inflating thrones, it evolved from cypherpunk whispers to global thunder. Laszlo's pizza etched its first value; halvings forged scarcity like digital Mount Olympus. Through Mt. Gox infernos, Silk Road storms, and nation-state sieges, Bitcoin rose unbowed.

Today, it crowns the sovereign individual, laser-eyes ablaze, hurtling toward a satoshi-standard world. The revolution is alive—decentralize or die!
